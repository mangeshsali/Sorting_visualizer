Quick sort complexity


Now, let's see the time complexity of quicksort in best case, average case, and in worst case.
We will also see the space complexity of quick sort.


1. Time Complexity

Best Case :- O(n*logn)
Average Case :- O(n*logn)
Worst Case :- O(n2)
